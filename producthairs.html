BEST SHAMPOO AND CONDITIONER FOR DRY HAIR Herbal Essences BioRenew Hydrate Coconut Milk Shampoo and Conditioner

Consumer testers loved these infusions, too: “My dry hair was much more moisturized,” one tester marveled.

BEST LIGHTWEIGHT SHAMPOO Garnier Whole Blends Legendary Olive Replenishing Shampoo

It may be blended with olive oil and argan oil, but Garnier's Legendary Olive shampoo along with its conditioner was a GH Beauty Lab test winner for its nourishing cleansing with no heaviness.
